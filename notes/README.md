## June 19, 2026 — Origin

This project started during a transatlantic flight (ZRH → LAX). Jason watched a TED talk on HASEL actuators after seeing a Unitree G1 humanoid robot and noting the danger problem. The conversation led here.

The two motivations are:
1. Bringing AI into the physical world in a form that is safe and human-scale
2. Solving the in-home care gap for people who need physical assistance

The connection between soft robotics and AI is not just technical — it is ethical. The question is not only *can* we build this, but *why would we*, and *for whom*.
